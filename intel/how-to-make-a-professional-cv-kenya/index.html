How to Make a Professional CV That Gets Interviews

Category: Career Intel · CVs · Job Search · Kenya & Global

Most CVs don’t fail because the candidate is unqualified. They fail because the CV is poorly structured, visually outdated, or unreadable by ATS systems.

This intel breaks down exactly how to make a professional CV using Canva, the same structure recruiters and hiring managers expect in 2026.

What Makes a CV “Professional” Today?

  • Clean layout with clear hierarchy
  • ATS-friendly formatting (no broken columns)
  • Results-focused bullet points, not job descriptions
  • Modern typography and spacing
  • Easy to scan in under 10 seconds

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best CV format in Kenya?

A reverse-chronological, ATS-friendly CV with clean sections performs best across Kenyan and global employers.

Can I use Canva CV templates for job applications?

Yes, as long as the template is structured correctly and exported as a PDF.

How long should a professional CV be?

One page for early career professionals, two pages maximum for experienced roles.
