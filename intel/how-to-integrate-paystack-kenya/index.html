How to Integrate Paystack in Kenya

Paystack allows Kenyan businesses to receive payments from anyone, anywhere. This guide shows you how to integrate it properly without breaking your system.

What This Solves

  • Receiving online payments
  • Accepting cards and mobile money
  • Automating confirmations

What You Need Before Starting

  • Registered business (BN or Ltd)
  • Valid email and phone number
  • Website or landing page

Step-by-Step Integration

  1. Create a Paystack account
  2. Verify your business
  3. Generate API keys
  4. Connect to your site or payment page

Common Mistakes

  • Using test keys in production
  • Not setting webhooks
  • Ignoring settlement rules
