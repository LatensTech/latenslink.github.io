About Us

Latenslink is a digital operations and systems partner in Kenya.

We help growing businesses move from scattered tools and memory-based operations to structured systems that are easy to run and easy to scale.

Our work is practical. We do not stop at strategy slides. We configure the tools, organize workflows, assign ownership, and make sure your team can use what we build.

Most issues we solve are operational: leads getting lost, inboxes unmanaged, follow-ups forgotten, tasks unclear, and reporting delayed. We build systems that reduce this friction.

What We Actually Do

  • Business Email & Workspace Setup
  • Lead Tracking & CRM Setup
  • Task & Project Management Setup
  • AI & Knowledge System Setup
  • Digital Operations Audit
  • Custom System Implementation
